Djamel Ouddane

My background is rooted in illustration and tattooing where I previously worked as a Tattoo artist for 5 years.

I enjoyed doing this art piece because it allowed me to bring together the things I am passionate about through developing both creative and coding practices.

My final piece involves real time images of tattoos. Channelling them into a geometric shape, creating an evolving kaleidoscope mandala.
