Elliot Brown

Elliot studied an Art and Design Foundation diploma at Manchester School of Art before coming to Goldsmiths to study Digital Arts Computing. This choice was to develop his interests in grids and shifts in perspective and interpreting space primarily using video and photography combined with other technologies in order to express this
He has a growing interest in interactive work and is incorporating this in to his work allowing his viewers to explore spaces differently.
‘The space that brought us here’ is a piece that challenges audiences perception of their surroundings in a more intimate way, though the physical engagement with the piece itself. Screens are used to show sections of the same space. The viewer is able to then reorientate the screens, through movement, creating new-found compositions
