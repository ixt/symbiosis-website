Emily Crosby

I am a 20 year old student from Cambridge who is currently studying BSc Digital Arts Computing at Goldsmiths.

Last year I worked on a projection piece which came from the concept of breaking free from captivity and returning to normal life. It was a projection showing birds flying into the screen onto washing lines, once a movement was detected all the birds would fly away. It was an animation created using Processing.js using a webcam to detect any movement.

For Symbiosis, my artwork will explore the patterns, shapes and forms that exist in nature. I will experiment with this using animation and image. I will be displaying a video piece that will use my own images, computer generated images and animations. The animations will randomly appear on the screen and will slowly start to grow onto the screen, which will have a random display of images collaged together.
