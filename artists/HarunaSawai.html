Haruna Sawai

Art and computing has always been a massive part of my life. I was accepted into the foundation degree of Computing at Goldsmiths to strengthen my computing skills. After completing the year I found that I was more interested in the combination of Art and Computing, especially Visual Art Computing.
I mostly enjoy producing generative drawings by using Processing.js, and creating simple and playful animations that are interactive.

For this exhibition I will be creating projection mapping to project programmed animations onto cuboid objects. By using specialised software, the object is specifically mapped on the cuboid surfaces and project the animation onto the surface.
