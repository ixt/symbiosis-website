Jonathan Laguna

I am a computational artist form South East London. I work with graphics, some of which have been used for promoting the art department at Lambeth College in London. Currently I am experimenting with motion graphics, inspired by computational artist John Whitney. I am exploring his idea in regards to design and motion, in which the use of numerical variables dominate a particular graphic patter. I will be showcasing the outcome of a series of interesting experiments I have created while studying and playing with numerical variables to create motions graphics and static graphic prints.
