Kotryna Saje

Little blonde girl that is still thinking should she define herself as an artist, programmer or just a dreamer. Brought herself to the huge metropolitan city of London from a small country in the middle of nowhere. Photography was and remains my main passion, but creating and developing ideas with both art and programming is also a thing I love to do. This time I will introduce light and reflection with a code and photography combination. Code helps me draw and project various patterns and textures on people to create physiognomies and different moods to show how you can play and lie with the light.
