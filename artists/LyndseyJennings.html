Lyndsey Jennings

I am a practising artist currently living in surrey and studying in London with a background in fine art. I am interested in the idea of geometric abstraction and how this contrasts to the biomorphic and the aesthetic relationship between the two. I mostly use flat surfaces and also use depth perception within my work to give the illusion that the piece operates in a more three dimensional space. With the current piece I am working on I am using a mixture of generative drawing, coding and practical materials to create a two dimensional work with the aesthetics that I intend. By using coding to create these shapes I can also refer to the digitally sublime by creating seemly endless layers of pattern.
