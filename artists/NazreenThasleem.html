Nazreen Thasleem

Art has always been a hobby of mine, it allows me to express my creativity with a story to tell and a vision to portray. I am an artist based in East London, my work is a constant search for the best way to interpret ideas about myself and my surroundings. I do not limit myself to one medium, form, style or concept. I have been motivated by other artists’ methods and techniques and have applied these in my artworks with new concepts. The piece I will be showcasing has been inspired by “Shadow Monsters” by Philip Worthington. It will consist of shadow art formed by the use of hand gestures accompanied with small movements. These shadows will be displayed on screen via story modes that I will initiate. The piece should allow the viewer to capture their inner child by creating their own shadow scene, allowing themselves to reminisce and cherish the memories of childhood.
