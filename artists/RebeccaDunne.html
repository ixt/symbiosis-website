Rebecca Dunne

My name is Rebecca and I am a 22 year old human from London.

When I was 16, I fell into a dark hole and found art. Art was a great coping mechanism, a rope and an extra limb. I also got an internet connection. Two addictions grew.

Since then, I have continuously worked on creative projects and have exhibited in various London locations, including Islington, Brentford and Chiswick. Within my work, I explore themes such as psychology, perception of the self, self-esteem, the body and the contrast between darkness and light, all filtered through my own life experiences.
